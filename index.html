HOW TO PLAY

Swipe left/right — change lane.

Swipe up — jump over low barriers.

Swipe down — slide under high barriers.

Tap SHOOT — fire your sidearm at drones ahead in your lane.

Hold GRAVITY — flip gravity and ride the ceiling to dodge floor hazards. Watch the green meter — it drains while flipped and refills on the floor.

Some hazards only exist on the ceiling — don't stay flipped too long!

Collect cyan data orbs for score & credits, amber ammo cells, and green gravity cells.

You have 3 shield hearts. Losing all of them ends the run.
